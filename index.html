About Bump & Grind R&B Party

Halifax's Only Dedicated R&B Experience ❤️

Bump & Grind is Halifax's favourite and original R&B party. Established in 2016, we have taken this event from intimate venues and rooftop patios to sold-out nights on some of the city's biggest stages. Sign up for our weekly newsletter to stay connected, get first dibs on tickets, and we'll see you at the next one.

"Not just a party. A weekly reason to fall back in love with R&B."
