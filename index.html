About Bump & Grind R&B Party

Halifax's Only Dedicated R&B Experience 🎶

Bump & Grind is Halifax's favourite and original R&B party. Established in 2016 — from intimate venues and rooftop patios to sold-out nights at some of the city's biggest stages. Stay connected with our weekly R&B newsletter and we'll see you at the next one. ❤️
